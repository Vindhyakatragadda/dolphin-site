Strategies Of D.Dolphin Company To Succeed In Franchising.
 • Understand and Protect Your Brand:
 It's essential to protect your brand and other intellectual property when franchising internationally.
This includes trademarks, copyrights, and patents.
• Plan Territories and Network Effectively:
 Carefully plan territories and network, considering regional
versus national expansion. This involves understanding how ready your fashion brand is for franchising and
how scalable it is for expansion
.• Develop a franchise business plan:
 Outline your franchise model, including franchise fees, royalties, and
support services.
• Improve Franchise Brand Consistency:
Establishing brand consistency across all franchise locations is
vital. This can be achieved through digital asset management systems, trigger marketing campaigns, and
regular audits of digital channels for compliance.
• Recruit and train the franchisees:
 Advertise your franchise opportunity and recruit potential franchisees through various channels, such as
franchise expos, social media, and online directories.
•   Monitor franchisee performance:
Regularly monitor franchisee performance and provide feedback to help them improve their operations.
• Renew franchise agreements:
 Renew franchise agreements with successful franchisees and terminate agreements with underperforming
franchisees.
